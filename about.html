I'm Diana

I’m a freelance UI/UX designer with a background in graphic design and advertising. I've always had a passion for art and design, and I'm lucky enough to have turned that passion into a career.

I graduated from APEC University in Santo Domingo, with a degree in Advertising. After graduation, I worked for 6 years as a graphic designer in several advertising agencies, designing logos, banners, billboards, adds, brochures, magazines, POPs, and stands.

In 2020, I decided to strike out on and started learning UI/UX design, and landed a job as campaign design specialist / QA on a tech company. I there I learned a lot about wireframe design and my knowledge on multi-brand management came up handy as I design email templates for small businesses.

When I'm not working, you can find me playing music, drawing, practicing yoga, or curled up with a good book. I'm also an avid traveler and love exploring new cultures and cuisines.

Thanks for stopping by my website. I'm always looking for new opportunities and collaborations, so please don't hesitate to get in touch!
